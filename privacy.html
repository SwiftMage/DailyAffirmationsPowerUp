Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2024

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information when you use the Daily Affirmations Power Up! app.

Information We Collect

The Daily Affirmations Power Up! app itself **does not collect or store any personal information** directly from its users. We do not require you to provide any personal data to use the core features of the app.

However, if you choose to subscribe to premium features, we use a third-party service called **RevenueCat** to manage subscriptions and purchase information. RevenueCat collects the following information linked to an anonymous identifier they generate for you:

  • **Purchase History:** Information related to your subscription status and purchase transactions. This is associated with an anonymous User ID generated by RevenueCat and cannot be used to personally identify you by us.

Data Usage

Since we do not collect personal information directly, we do not use it.

The anonymous purchase information collected by RevenueCat is used for the following purposes:

  • **App Functionality:** To authenticate your subscription status, enable premium features you've purchased, prevent fraud, implement security measures, ensure server up-time, minimize app crashes, improve scalability and performance, or perform customer support related to your subscription.
  • **Analytics:** To evaluate user behavior related to subscriptions (e.g., understand the effectiveness of features, plan new features, or measure audience size for subscription tiers). This data is anonymous and helps us improve the app.

Children's Privacy

Daily Affirmations Power Up! is designed for children and is safe to use. We do not directly collect any personal information from children or any other users. The anonymous identifier collected by RevenueCat for subscription management is not used to personally identify children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other relevant privacy regulations.

Third-Party Services

We use **RevenueCat** to manage in-app subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat generates an anonymous identifier to link your device to your subscription status. RevenueCat has its own privacy policy detailing how they handle data. You can review RevenueCat's privacy policy here: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@powerwordsapp.com.
