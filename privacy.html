Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2024

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information when you use the Power Words app.

Information We Collect

The Power Words app **does not collect or store any personal information** from its users. We do not require you to provide any personal data to use the app.

Data Usage

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use, share, or store any user data.

Children's Privacy

Power Words is designed for children and is safe to use. We do not collect any personal information from children or any other users. The app fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other relevant privacy regulations.

Third-Party Services

The Power Words app does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect information from you.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@powerwordsapp.com.
